The Story So Far

There used to be a player on this server who wanted control.

Not attention. Not wealth. Control.

He was not strong at first. He was not even important. But he was patient.

While everyone else focused on building and fighting, he went looking for something deeper.

That search led him to the Nether.

Somewhere below the usual structures, he found something that should not have been there: an orb. It reacted to nearby deaths. It stored energy.

He experimented.

At first he tried using what was already in the Nether. Then what was left behind in the End.

It barely worked.

Eventually he figured it out.

The strongest energy did not come from the dead.

It came from the living.

So he came back to the Overworld and started testing.

Every battle. Every death. Every conflict made the orb stronger.

And as it grew, so did he.

He learned how to bind undead mobs.
He learned how to manipulate players.
He stopped asking for land and started taking it.

The more power he pulled from others, the less stable he became.

The orb was not just storing souls.

It was changing him.

He got paranoid. Aggressive. Obsessed.

When people tried to stop him, he did not negotiate.

He escalated.

Eventually the rest of the server realized he could not just be killed. Every time he was close to losing, the orb reacted.

So they did something else.

They sealed him underground.

Buried the orb with him.

Locked both away.

Not destroyed.

Just contained.

And now the seal is not holding the way it used to.

The orb is active again.

And if someone finds it, we will see whether they can use it...

or whether it uses them.
